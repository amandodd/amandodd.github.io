ABOUT ME

DODD

Hi, I’m Aman, a 19-year old developer in my second year of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I’m currently skilled in Java, C++, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Furthermore, I’ve always liked to classify myself as a continuous learner; someone who’s always searching to surpass the personal knowledge boundaries and learn more.

To me, life as a whole is an investment in a process of finding your niche, your passion, and I can rightfully say I was a late-bloomer in that category. Prior to being in CS, I partook in two years of Mechanical Engineering (if you haven’t noticed already), before making the move over to CS. It wasn't so I hated Mechanical Engineering, it was more so the potential and growth I saw in myself, which I believe could only be fulfilled in a path of passion which for me was Computer Science; a field not only which I enjoyed immensely, but one that is growing hastily.
